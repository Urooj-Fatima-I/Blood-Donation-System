## **Overview**
The Asaan Blood Donation Management System is a console-based C++ application developed to manage blood donors and blood inventory efficiently. It allows users to register eligible donors, search donor records by blood group, and perform blood donation and request operations with proper input validation. The system also generates summary reports showing donation statistics and low blood stock alerts, helping ensure organized data management and timely availability of blood.

---

## **Features**
- **Register new blood donors** with eligibility checking  
- **View donor list** stored in the system  
- **Search donors by blood group**  
- **Donate blood** and update inventory  
- **Request blood** based on availability  
- **Generate summary reports**  
- **Low blood stock notifications**  

---

## Menu Options

### **Press 1 – Add a Donor**
- Registers a new donor after checking age and recent donation history.  
- Validates donor details including name, blood group, and phone number.  
- Saves the donor information to the system for future reference.  

### **Press 2 – View Donor List**
- Displays the full list of all donors registered in the system.  
- Shows donor name, blood group, and contact information in a formatted layout.  
- Allows quick access to all donor records for review or management.  

### **Press 3 – Search Donor**
- Lets the user search for donors by specifying a blood group.  
- Displays matching donor details if available in the system.  
- Helps quickly find compatible donors for urgent blood requirements.  

### **Press 4 – View Summary**
- Generates a summary report of all donation activities.  
- Shows total donors added, blood donated and requested, and type-wise statistics.  
- Alerts the user about blood types that are low in stock for timely action.  

### **Press 5 – Donate or Request Blood**
- Allows the user to either donate blood or request blood from inventory.  
- Updates the blood stock automatically based on the operation.  
- Keeps track of donations and requests for accurate reporting.  

### **Press 6 – About Us**
- Provides information about the system’s purpose and objectives.  
- Explains how the system connects donors, recipients, and blood banks efficiently.  
- Highlights the goal of promoting voluntary blood donation and saving lives.  

### **Press 7 – Exit Program**
- Safely terminates the application.  
- Ensures that all data is saved before exiting.  
- Confirms a smooth and secure closure of the system.
